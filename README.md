# End-to-End-Data-Analysis-Portfolio

This repository showcases a collection of end-to-end data analytics and machine learning projects focused on solving real-world business problems. Each project demonstrates data cleaning, exploratory analysis, visualization, and predictive modeling.

---

## Projects Included

### 1. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) Prediction

* **Objective:** Predict the future value of customers based on purchasing behavior.
* **Techniques Used:** Feature Engineering (Recency, Frequency, Monetary), Regression Models
* **Key Insights:**

  * High-frequency customers generate more long-term value
  * Customer segmentation helps in targeted marketing strategies

---

### 2. Retail Business Performance Analysis

* **Objective:** Analyze sales and profit trends to identify high-performing and loss-making areas.
* **Techniques Used:** Data Cleaning, Aggregation, Correlation Analysis, Visualization
* **Key Insights:**

  * Technology products drive the highest profit
  * Some categories have high sales but low profitability

---

### 3. HR Analytics – Employee Attrition Prediction

* **Objective:** Predict employee attrition and identify factors affecting employee retention.
* **Techniques Used:** Exploratory Data Analysis, Logistic Regression (Classification)
* **Key Insights:**

  * Low salary and overtime significantly increase attrition risk
  * Job roles and work-life balance impact employee retention

---

### 4. E-commerce Return Rate Analysis

* **Objective:** Analyze product return behavior and predict return probability.
* **Techniques Used:** Data Analysis, Logistic Regression
* **Key Insights:**

  * High-priced items tend to have higher return rates
  * Certain product categories are more prone to returns

---

### 5. Customer Churn Analysis

* **Objective:** Predict customer churn and identify customers at risk of leaving.
* **Techniques Used:** Data Cleaning, Exploratory Data Analysis, Classification Models (Logistic Regression / Decision Tree)
* **Key Insights:**

  * Customers with low engagement are more likely to churn
  * High service issues and low usage increase churn probability
  * Identifying at-risk customers helps in improving retention strategies
